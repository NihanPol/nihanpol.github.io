My research revolves around neutron stars. I'm particularly interested in double neutron star (DNS) systems, especially the 
Double Pulsar, J0737-3039 system and the J1757-1854 system. I'm also interested in detecting nanohertz gravitational waves
using pulsar timing arrays, which I do as a member of the North American Nanohertz Observatory for gravitational waves
(NANOGrav).
